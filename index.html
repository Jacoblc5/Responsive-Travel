Facts About Notre-Dame

  • Notre-Dame Cathedral was built in the 12th century and completed in the 13th century.
  • It is located on the Île de la Cité in the heart of Paris, France.
  • A devastating fire in April 2019 caused significant damage to the cathedral, leading to an ongoing restoration effort.
